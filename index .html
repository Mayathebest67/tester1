The Original Pancake House started in 1953 in Portland, Oregon. They used real ingredients and special recipes to make amazing pancakes.

The Apple Pancake is a big pancake with apples and cinnamon baked on top.

The Dutch Baby is a fluffy oven-baked pancake served with lemon, butter, and powdered sugar.

They also make omelets, crepes, and other breakfast foods using fresh ingredients.

They want every customer to have great food and great service.
